About This Project

Old Bike Shell conjures imagery of resilience and protection, suggesting a project that repurposes old motorcycles into meaningful assets. The word "shell" symbolizes both safety and sustainability — our core values.

By breathing new life into discarded bikes, this initiative promotes environmental conservation and affordable transportation. The project empowers communities to engage in responsible reuse and gain access to reliable mobility solutions.

This tool helps users predict the price of a used motorcycle based on its brand, ownership, power, age, and kilometers driven — allowing smart, data-driven purchase decisions.
